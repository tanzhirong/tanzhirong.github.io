Prediction of Maximum Heart Rate Attained during Exercise

I seek to understand a dataset of patients with certain heart and physical health conditions. The aim is to build a model for the response maxhr (maximum heart rate attained during exericse) based on the physical conditions of the participants. The data is obtained from Machine Learning Repository @ UCI.
