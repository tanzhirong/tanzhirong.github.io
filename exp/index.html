Work Experiences

Shopee Singapore
Product Management Intern - Listing & User Team
June - September 2018

During 3 months there, I owned 4 product features, from conceptualization and testing, to live deployment. I designed wireframes using HTML and CSS, and authored use cases to communicate feature requirements to stakeholders. Specific features include upgrading the platform’s category trees and attributes system, enhancing product listing pages through 2-tier variations and customization, and automating listing Quality Control solutions.

It was exhilarating witnessing the growth of one of Southeast Asia's largest ecommerce platforms. Part of the satisfaction came from working with talented people accross various departments, and collaborating with business teams in 7 countries to identify customers’ pain-points through market research and industry best practices.
